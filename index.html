About me

Hi I am Ayush Mishra a diligent and ambitious student currently pursuing my Bachelor of Vocational Studies (BVoc) degree at Indira Gandhi National Tribal University (IGNTU). I am always been passionate about technology, particularly in the realm of mobile devices, which led me to become an expert in phone repairing.

My journey in phone repairing began at a young age when I was fascinated by the intricate workings of mobile phones. This curiosity drove me to learn everything I could about mobile technology. I started by watching online tutorials, reading technical manuals, and practicing on old and discarded phones. My persistence and dedication paid off, and I soon became proficient in diagnosing and fixing a wide range of mobile device issues. Over the years, I have developed a comprehensive skill set in phone repairing. I'm adept at hardware repairs, including screen replacements, battery swaps, and fixing internal components such as cameras, speakers, and microphones. I'm also skilled in software troubleshooting, dealing with issues like firmware updates, software glitches, and malware removal. My ability to quickly and accurately diagnose problems and provide effective solutions has earned me a reputation as a reliable and knowledgeable phone repair expert.

Additionally, I am a front-end developer with knowledge of C and C++. This skill set allows me to understand and work on the software side of mobile technology, further enhancing my ability to provide comprehensive solutions to technical problems. My journey is a testament to hard work, passion, and dedication. As I continue to excel in my BVoc studies at IGNTU and refine my phone repairing and development skills, I am well on my way to making a significant impact in the world of mobile technology.
